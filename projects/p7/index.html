MASSERIA WAVE
[CORPORATE IDENTITY]

Masseria Wave is a unique format that emerged in the spring of 2020, transforming a historic farm in the heart of Salento, Puglia, into a vibrant cultural production center for creative digital nomads and international artists, with a special focus on queer culture and events. Each summer, it hosts a diverse program of events, including DJ sets, live music, performances, exhibitions, talks, workshops, drag shows, screenings, and much more. I began working with Masseria Wave in the spring of 2022, initially under the creative direction of Gabriele Corizzo, focusing on the corporate identity and visual communication.

Masseria Wave provided me with my first professional experience, allowing me to apply my academic knowledge with complete creative freedom.

Since 2022 until this year, I have been responsible for curating the entire visual communication, both online and offline.
