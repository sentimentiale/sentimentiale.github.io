"LA SCALA DEL CALCIO" MW T-SHIRT - Kappa x Calcetto Eleganza @Linecheck 2024, Milano
[APPAREL DESIGN]

"La Scala del Calcio" is a unique event that blends art and performance, featuring a tournament in collaboration with @kappa_sport. Four collectives will showcase their vision of football, both on and off the field: @trnstnradio, @masseria.wave, @linecheck_it, and @calcettoeleganza. Representing the third team, Masseria Wave, a queer festival and community space, stands out for its focus on artistic proliferation, self-determination, and creative vision.

I was responsible for designing the team’s t-shirt, capturing the essence of Masseria Wave’s identity in a wearable form.
