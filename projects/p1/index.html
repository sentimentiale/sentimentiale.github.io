WAVE ZINE - ISSUE 01 - THE OUT OF OFFICE
[VISUAL IDENTITY DESIGN]

An independent publication that brings together the work of more than 40 international artists on the theme Out Of Office: the need to reconsider one's time and living space in favor of a new and increasingly urgent well-being.

With different languages including photographs, illustrations, interviews, unpublished materials, tales of exploration and personal stories, fantastical stories, fake adv, futuristic-flavored photo stories, the contents are dedicated to burnout, return to the periphery, and toxic work.

Contributors include: Vincenzo Sparagna (Frigidaire, Frigolandia), Princess Julia, Lomography, Read Uncomfortable, Funclab Collective, Coeval, Ivan Carozzi, and many* others

THE OUT OF OFFICE encompasses the common need to get away from a poisoned everyday life, punctuated by exasperated rhythms and increasingly high performance causing burnout and job abandonment, in order to re-evaluate ecentralized and peripheral places, where time dilates, allowing us a reflection on ways to experience a new well-being.

The editorial result is an experimental process of bringing together a choral narrative within a single project. Part of the artistic contributions featured in Wave Zine were selected from the numerous works submitted by the public and artists* who responded to the open call promoted on Masseria Wave's social channels.
