"CERCO STANZA A MILANO" T-SHIRT
[APPAREL DESIGN]

The concept for "Cerco Stanza a Milano" was born from a widely shared insight in the city of Milan. This city, known for its design and fashion, attracts countless individuals eager to build connections and work towards their futures. However, they often find themselves "stuck" in a place where finding a living space has become nearly impossible. Sky-high rents and sometimes precarious living conditions create significant barriers. This project aims to highlight and bring awareness to the housing challenges faced by many in Milan, reflecting a critical issue through the lens of apparel design.
