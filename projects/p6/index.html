"DOLCE STUDIO" - THE LAB TALENTS
[VISUAL IDENTITY DESIGN]

For the vintage jewelry brand project commissioned by The Lab Talents, I developed a fictional brand identity under the name "Dolce Studio." I crafted a visual aesthetic that blends subtle elegance with timeless appeal, using a soft, refined color palette. The social media communication strategy was designed to be minimalistic yet impactful, ensuring alignment with the brief's directives. The result is a cohesive brand presence that resonates with the vintage essence while appealing to modern sensibilities.
